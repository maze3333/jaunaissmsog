THE FIRST SMS · 1992

In 1992, the very first text message travelled through the airwaves from a computer to a chunky Orbitel GSM handset. It simply read “Merry Christmas” – a small message that quietly started the era of SMS and everything that came after. $OGSMS is a tribute to that moment, freezing the original greeting forever on‑chain.
